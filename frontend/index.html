Investigator workflow

Five steps from intake to closure

A disciplined process designed for cybercrime units — OCR and AI assist at defined stages, never replacing investigator judgment.

  1. 1

    Open case & intake

    Create a case with crime type, tags, and assignee. Capture initial complaint details and link related references.

  2. 2

    Upload evidence

    Attach screenshots, chats, PDFs, and transaction records. Track upload status and chain-of-custody metadata.

  3. 3

    Review OCR extractions

    AI suggests phone numbers, UPI IDs, and account details. Investigators approve or edit each field before it enters the case graph.

  4. 4

    Confirm correlations & graph

    Review cross-case link suggestions, confirm relationships, and explore the entity graph for infrastructure patterns.

  5. 5

    Generate report & close

    Compile investigation-ready documentation with threat score factors, timeline, and analyst notes. Supervisor approval when required.
